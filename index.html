Frequently Asked Questions

Is the callout and fitment really free?

Yes. For customers within Alberton (including New Redruth, Meyersdal, and surrounding areas), our mobile callout, expert diagnosis, and professional fitment are completely free. No hidden costs.

What brands do you stock?

We are certified stockists for Raylite and Willard, South Africa's most trusted OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) battery brands. This ensures you get the exact quality your car manufacturer recommends.

What warranty do I get?

All our new batteries come with a full, nationally-supported manufacturer's warranty. You buy with complete peace of mind, knowing your battery is covered.

My car has Start-Stop. Can you help?

Absolutely. We are specialists in modern AGM and EFB batteries required for Start-Stop vehicles. We also have the diagnostic tools to correctly register the new battery with your car's computer (essential for BMW, Audi, etc.).

How quickly can you get to me?

Because we are hyper-local and based right here in New Redruth, we offer Alberton's fastest callout service. Contact us, and we will dispatch a technician immediately, often arriving within 30-60 minutes.

Do you just sell batteries?

No, we provide a full diagnostic service. Our free callout includes testing your battery, alternator, and starter to find the *real* problem. We won't sell you a battery you don't need.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 17:00, and on Saturdays from 08:00 to 13:00. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you sell inverter or bike batteries?

Yes. We stock a wide range of batteries for all applications, including deep cycle and high-cycle batteries for inverters, as well as specialist batteries for motorcycles, jet skis, and quad bikes.
